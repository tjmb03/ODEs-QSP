📊 Fisher's Exact Test — Literature Validation

Tests whether positive-feedback loops are non-randomly enriched among pathological outcomes in your literature corpus. H₀: feedback sign is independent of disease outcome. p < 0.05 → your extracted network has the expected biology; p > 0.05 → extraction may be missing key loops.
